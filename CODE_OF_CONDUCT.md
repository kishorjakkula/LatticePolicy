# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, productive, and harassment-free project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation details.
- Assume good intent, but accept responsibility for impact.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality, especially around security reports and customer-like sample data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Public disclosure of unresolved security vulnerabilities.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this code.
